My Story

I'm Muhammad Haekal, a graduate in Mechatronics Engineering from SMK SMTI Makassar with a passion for both technical problem-solving and creative photography. My journey began with a fascination for how things work, which led me to pursue mechatronics - a field that combines mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering.

During my internship at PT Surveyor Indonesia, I developed strong analytical skills working with environmental data and technical documentation. This experience taught me the importance of attention to detail and systematic approaches to problem-solving.

Parallel to my technical education, I discovered photography as a creative outlet. What started as a hobby quickly evolved into a professional pursuit, specializing in automotive photography. My technical background gives me a unique perspective when capturing the intricate details of vehicles.

I believe the combination of my technical expertise and creative vision allows me to approach challenges from multiple perspectives, whether it's analyzing data or composing the perfect shot.
